About Private Labeling

At Vimson Derma, we specialize in private label manufacturing of skincare, haircare, baby care, and personal care products, enabling businesses to launch their own brand effortlessly. Skip the R&D hassle—our ready-to-brand, dermatologist-approved formulations let you launch fast and look good doing it. From anti-acne serums to hair repair heroes, every product is crafted in-house at our certified Gujarat facility with a focus on quality, safety, and compliance. Ideal for salons, clinics, retailers, and beautypreneurs, our private label model means you pick it, brand it, and we’ll handle the rest.

Why choose our Private Label Services?

Ready-to-Launch Product Range

Skip R&D and development time. Choose from our 200+ proven, market-ready trendy skincare, haircare, and personal care products and launch quickly under your brand name.

Low Investment, High Speed

Private labels are perfect for brands that want to move fast with limited budgets. No need to invest in formulation—just pick, brand, and launch.

Brand Ownership Without Complexity

Want to launch a summer range or festival edition quickly? Private labels give you the flexibility to introduce new products with minimal lead time.
