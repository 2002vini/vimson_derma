Overview

At Vimson Derma, we specialize in helping brands create products that truly represent their identity. Whether you're an emerging entrepreneur or an established label, we offer end-to-end customization in skincare, haircare, bodycare, baby care, intimate care, and more excluding medicated products where standard, compliant formulations are followed.

From the choice of active ingredients to packaging aesthetics, every element is customizable to meet your vision and market demands.

Customization Options

Formulation

 Create unique blends with custom ingredients, skin-safe claims like vegan and sulfate-free, and globally inspired formulations.

Fragrances

Choose from floral, herbal, musky, fruity, or opt for fragrance-free options to suit sensitive skin.

Packaging

Choose from materials (glass, PET), designs (minimalistic, luxury), and sizes.

Texture And Color

Define the texture and color of your product with options ranging from lightweight gels to rich creams and soft neutrals to bold tones.
