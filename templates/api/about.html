Your Trusted Partner in Private Label & Third-Party Cosmetic Manufacturing

Crafting Beauty, Delivering Trust

Welcome to Vimson Derma, a name trusted by Cosmeceutical Company, skincare entrepreneurs, and global cosmetic brands. As a leading third-party manufacturer of cosmetic and medicated products in India, we offer custom skincare solutions, private label manufacturing, and end-to-end product development services, all under one roof.

Founded in 2014 by Mr. Vimal Bachhawat and Mr. Kalpesh Bachhawat, Vimson Derma was built on a vision to combine science, beauty, and business and today, we are proud to be ranked among the Top 30 Contract Manufacturers and Suppliers of Medicated Products in India.

Backed by 50+ years of industry experience,

we specialize in skincare, haircare, dermatology and pharma-cosmetic products that are trusted across markets. From low MOQs for startups to scalable production for global brands, we make launching and growing your brand easy, efficient, and profitable.
