About Third Party Manafacturing

At Vimson Derma, we offer end-to-end skincare and cosmetic manufacturing solutions, from research & development to high-quality production. With FDA-approved facilities, advanced formulations, and a commitment to safety and efficacy, we ensure premium, customized products tailored to your brand’s needs.

Why Choose our third party Manafacturing?

Custom formulations tailored to your brand’s vision
Full control over ingredients and product design
Advanced R&D and testing capabilities.
Large-scale production for growing businesses.

Product Brief & Custom Requirement Gathering
Every great product starts with a strong brief and we’re all ears. We dive deep to understand your brand’s vision, target market, and formulation goals. Whether it’s a sulfate-free shampoo, an anti-pigmentation cream, a ceramide-based hair perfume, or a scalp treatment serum, we create a tailored roadmap.

R&D and Formulation Development
Our in-house R&D lab in Gujarat, led by expert cosmetic chemists, crafts personalized skincare and haircare formulations using science-backed and trending actives. From peptides and ceramides to snail mucin, fermented botanicals, and niacinamide - your formula is developed for performance and uniqueness.
03
Click here
Sampling, Testing & Regulatory Compliance
Once your formulation is whipped up, we send you lab samples for testing and review. We assist with dermatological testing (optional), ingredient disclosures, label claims, and compliance with Indian and global cosmetic regulations.
04
Click here
Purchase Order, Sales Order & Bulk Manufacturing at WHO-GMP Certified Facility
Once you're happy with the sample and ready to roll, we lock things in with a Purchase Order (PO) from your side and generate a Sales Order (SO) from ours. Manufacturing then begins at our WHO-GMP certified plant in Gujarat with strict quality checks in place. We accommodate flexible batch sizes (starting at 3,000 units) and have the scale to grow with your brand.
05
Click here
Packaging, Labeling, QC & Final Dispatch
Bring your own packaging, or let us connect you with our trusted vendors for jars, tubes, bottles, and droppers, whatever suits your brand aesthetic. Post-final QC, products are batch-coded, shrink-wrapped, labeled, and made ready for dispatch.
